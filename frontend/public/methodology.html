Quality Control

Data is only as good as its quality control. We cross-check every source, validate every coordinate, and test every algorithm. If we're not confident in a data point, we don't use it.

Technology Stack

  • React.js with interactive maps and visualizations
  • Python pipelines for data processing
  • Google Gemini 2.5 Pro for AI analysis
  • Advanced statistics and time series analysis
  • Automated testing and deployment

What We Can't Do

Honest limitations matter. Our data can't:

  • Predict individual incidents
  • Prove causation (only correlation)
  • Account for unreported incidents
  • Show immediate policy effects
  • Include all private transactions

Bottom line: We're a powerful tool for understanding trends, not a crystal ball. Use our insights as part of broader evidence, not the final word.
