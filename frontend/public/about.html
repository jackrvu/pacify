Why This Matters

Gun violence policy is often driven by politics, not evidence. We provide the missing piece: clear, unbiased analysis of what actually works to prevent violence.

  • Help lawmakers make evidence-based decisions
  • Show communities what prevention works
  • Give researchers powerful analysis tools
  • Make complex data accessible to everyone
  • Turn statistics into actionable insights

The Technology

We use cutting-edge AI and machine learning to process over 500,000 incidents. Our platform correlates data with policy changes and presents insights anyone can understand.

  • AI-powered analysis using Google's latest technology
  • Real-time data processing and updates
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Academic rigor, user-friendly interface
  • Open, transparent methodology
